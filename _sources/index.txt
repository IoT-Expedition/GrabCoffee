GiottoDataViewer for iOS
=========================

.. image:: ./images/web_interface.png
   :height: 400px

.. image:: ./images/humidity.png
   :height: 400px

GiottoDataViwer is an iOS app that allows you to search Building Depot sensors around you,
and see visualization of sensor readings from the sensors. To use this app, you should have
installed and property configured `Building Depot`_.

.. _`Building Depot`: http://www.buildingdepot.org
